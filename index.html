Tempered Glass

Undergoing the thermal tempering process, tempered glass acquires such characteristics as toughness, resistance to cold-heat fluctuations, scratch-protection and safety. These are the reasons why tempered glass is a number one choice for a variety of purposes: building windows, glass tables, frameless shower doors, any installations where safety considerations are regarded.
